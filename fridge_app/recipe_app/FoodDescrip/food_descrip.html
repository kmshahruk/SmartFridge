Country Ham Sandwiches

A simple and healthy meal to satisfy your hunger.

Total time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

2 slices of ham 2 slices of bread 1 tbsp of butter 2 slices of cheese 2 lettuce leaves 2 tbsp of mayonnaise Add to Shopping List

Preparation

Heat up the pan for 1 minute. Then place a tbsp on the pan and toast the 2 slices of
bread for 1 minute.

Time: 2 minutes Once the breads are toasted, place it on a plate and layer one slice of bread with ham,
cheese, and lettuce.

Time: 1 minutes Spread the mayonnaise on the other slice of bread.

Time: 1 minutes Stack the bread with mayonnaise on the layered pile. Bon Appetit!

Time: 1 minutes
